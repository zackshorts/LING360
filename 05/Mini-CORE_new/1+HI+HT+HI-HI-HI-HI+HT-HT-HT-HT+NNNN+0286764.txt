<h> Understanding How To: Apply to Use or Buy Crown Land
<p> British Columbia is the third largest province in Canada and covers a total area of 94.8 million hectares (948000 square KM). B.C. is nearly four times the size of Great Britain and larger than any American state except Alaska.
<p> Crown land is land that is owned by the Province of British Columbia. The Province is the steward of the land and can lease, license and sell Crown land for a variety of purposes including residential, commercial, industrial, agricultural, tourism and recreational purposes.
<h> Other Resources
<p> Land Title Survey Authority is available for registering land titles as well as searching for land titles, conservation covenants, riparian rights and other related land title and land survey information
